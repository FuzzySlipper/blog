## Executive Summary

Between June 22–30, 2026, *Love and Deepspace* (恋与深空) — Papergames' (叠纸) otome mobile game with nearly $1.1B in global cumulative revenue — experienced its worst trust crisis since launch. A cascade of four overlapping controversies erupted: the sudden "parachute drop" of a 6th male lead Ao Yin (敖尹), the discovery of human experimentation file code "A-0731" (perceived as referencing Imperial Japan's Unit 731), promotional copy "inviting the wolf into the house" (引狼入室) publicly condemned by the All-China Women's Federation newspaper, and "don't bathe in the pot" copy linked to a real domestic violence murder case.

The developer issued three official responses in ten days: the first was derided as "sneak-attack PR," the second as "evasive and insubstantial," and the third (night of June 30) amounted to full capitulation — **cancelling Ao Yin entirely, pledging never to add another male lead, and offering 30 free pulls as compensation.** By July 1, the game had lost ~1.23 million followers across platforms, App Store ratings had cratered to 1.6–1.8 (from 7+), TapTap to 2.0–2.5, with over 60,000 complaints on consumer platforms and 40,000+ participants in collective complaints.

The fundamental issue: Papergames operated the game as a *content product* while players invested in it as an *emotional home*. When the developer treated a new character as a commercial expansion tool while leaving existing characters' storylines un-updated for 500+ days, the implicit emotional contract shattered.

## 2. Event Timeline (June 22–30, 2026)

### Phase 1: The Drop (June 22)

**June 22** — Love and Deepspace abruptly announced a 6th romanceable male lead "Ao Yin" (敖尹) via livestream, accompanied by giant beast footprints laid outside Papergames' real-world office building. Character concept: werewolf + corporate chairman dual identity, Western hard-masculine art style, English name Valko (Slavic for "wolf").

**Player outrage — core grievances:**

| Dimension | Player Allegation |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Aesthetic rupture** | Western hard-masculine style clashes severely with the game's established bright, delicate "Papergames house style"; dubbed "ugliest otome male lead ever" |
| **Zero narrative buildup** | Previous male leads (Qin Che, Xia Yizhou) all had extended main-storyline foreshadowing before debut; Ao Yin "parachuted" with no worldbuilding integration |
| **Broken promise** | Official channels had repeatedly implied a fixed five-lead roster; customer service explicitly stated "no new romanceable characters in the near term" |
| **Production anxiety** | Five existing leads' storylines un-updated 500+ days, yet the developer "secretly made a sixth guy" |
| **Gacha dilution** | Permanent banner expanded from 5 to 6 characters, lowering probability of pulling one's preferred lead |
| **Identity/lore problems** | Players dug up that Ao Yin's EonCore Group may be the幕后主使 (puppet master) behind the game's villain organization EVER — making him potentially "the source of all the other male leads' suffering" |

**Data eruption:**
- Official announcement Weibo post: 1M+ comments (overwhelmingly negative)
- June 22–26: ~500K followers lost across platforms
- Consumer protection platform collective complaints: 40,000+ participants
- Single-day complaint peak: 21,961 (June 23)

### Phase 2: Cascade Detonation (June 25–29)

**June 25** — Livestream countdown promotional copy: "absolutely don't bathe in the pot" (绝对不要在锅里洗澡). Players linked this to the 2024 Shaanxi Baishui iron-pot wife-killing domestic violence case. Official later stated it was "rule-horror style预热 (teaser) for the Home 2.0 kitchen feature" and deleted all related materials.

**June 27** — *Beijing News* (新京报) published "Six Questions for Love and Deepspace: Consumers Aren't Waiting for a Vague Response," raising six consumer-rights challenges (false advertising standards, right to know, refund mechanisms). Multiple outlets followed.

**June 28, early AM** — First official statement. Admitted "insufficient communication," offered 20 wish tickets as compensation, but explicitly stated Ao Yin would NOT be cancelled. The凌晨 (pre-dawn) timing was denounced as "sneak-attack PR." Nearly 300K comments, predominantly "not accepted" and "you addressed zero things."

**June 28, daytime** — PV "Law of Instinctive Surrender" (本能沦陷法则) released. The line "What's wrong with inviting the wolf into the house?" (引狼入室有什么不好?) paired with footage of the character breaking into a woman's home ignited the second wave.

**June 28** — **China Women's News** (中国妇女报, newspaper of the All-China Women's Federation) published a named condemnation: *"Packaging boundary violation as romance, rationalizing the illegal act of home invasion… Games can be fantastical, but cannot脱离公序良俗 (depart from public order and good customs), and certainly cannot trade women's sense of personal safety for hype."*

**June 29** — **#恋与深空731#** topped Weibo trending. Players discovered that in the April 21 "World Depths" (世界深处) update, a human experimentation file bore the code **"A-0731"** — in the context of live-subject experimentation剧情 (plot content), this was perceived as a direct reference to Imperial Japan's Unit 731 atrocities.

**Parallel "coincidence" allegations:**

| Discovery | Player Interpretation |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Experiment file code "A-0731" | References Unit 731 |
| Registrar name "Yan Song" (严颂) | Homophone for Class-A war criminal Iwamatsu Yoshio (岩松义雄) |
| Ao Yin's English name "Valko" | Phonetic联想 (association) with "wōkòu" (倭寇, a historical Chinese slur for Japanese pirates) |
| Founder Yao Runhao | Graduated from Waseda University, Japan |

**June 29, night** — Second official statement. Core messaging:
- 0731 was a "fictional date, meaningless placeholder," will be changed (to 0611)
- "引狼入室" text will be changed to "纯爱狼王" (Pure-Love Wolf King)
- Denies rumors of "overseas-exclusive character" and "game becoming all-orientation"
- Claims "organized fabrication and dissemination of disinformation," threatens legal action

**Player response:** The replacement date 0611 was immediately flagged as the start date of the Battle of Wuhan (June 11, 1938) and the Daojiao June 11 Incident (Japanese poison gas attack). Verdict: "They changed it and it's STILL offensive."

### Phase 3: Full Capitulation (June 30)

**June 30, night** — Third official statement ("A Letter to All Players"). Key commitments:

> - **Cancel Ao Yin's launch and development completely**
> - **Pledge never to introduce another new male lead**
> - Entire team to focus on the five existing leads, stabilize main-story production cadence
> - July: Qi Yu new solar card; September: Xia Yizhou main-story branch; November: Qin Che main-story branch
> - 30 consecutive days of login rewards: 30 free wish tickets
> - Originally paid content (outfits, photo poses, scenes, 缄夜 gift box) to be given free

**Divided player response:**
- Some: "clear stance, willing to return"
- Others: "emotional-manipulation statement, no real introspection on the problems"
- Refund channel demand remains unanswered
- As of June 30, 21:00, Ao Yin promotional materials remained live on multiple platforms despite cancellation

## 3. Legal Dimension

### 3.1 Papergames as Plaintiff: Pact Defamation Case (VICTORY)

A significant legal win that forms an interesting "offense/defense duality" with the June crisis:

- **Aug 2024:** Rapper Pact (派克特, real name Wang Kun, 2.02M followers) released diss track "Unscrupulous Game" (无良游戏), using terms like "poisoning minors" and "sleazy/sexually suggestive" to attack Love and Deepspace
- **Dec 2024:** Papergames filed defamation lawsuit
- **April 14, 2026:** Shanghai No. 2 Intermediate People's Court final judgment (Case No. (2026)沪02民终839号) — **Papergames wins**

**Key holdings:**
- **First case establishing that virtual characters' reputation rights are legally protected** — virtual characters, as derivative creative works, enjoy legal standing against malicious disparagement
- Pact, as a public figure with 2M+ followers, bears heightened duty of care; "using criticism of minor protection as a pretext for abusive expression" is not legally shielded
- Ordered to post a pinned public apology on social platforms for 3 consecutive days within 10 days of judgment

**Enforcement deadlock:** Pact has refused to comply past the deadline. Papergames has initiated compulsory enforcement proceedings (court-ordered publication of judgment, fines, or placement on the dishonesty blacklist).

### 3.2 Papergames as Defendant: Player Lawyer Letters & Potential Class Action

- **Aug 2025:** Shen Xinghui "Eclipse King" solar card — PV showed mechanics (energy-bar instant recovery, crowd-control pull, environmental-attack immunity clearing) that were entirely absent from the live version. Players委托 (retained) lawyers to issue formal demand letters seeking "refund ×3" (退一赔三) under consumer protection law
- **June 2026:** Refund demands resurface as a central issue during the Ao Yin crisis. Black Cat (黑猫) complaints: 60,000+. Consumer protection collective complaints: 40,000+
- **Legal frontier question:** Whether game PV false advertising falls under the Advertising Law and Consumer Rights Protection Law "refund ×3" provisions is a novel legal question; whether courts would accept a class action remains untested

## 6. Deep Analysis: Why This Time Was Different

### 6.1 "Emotional Contract" vs. "Content Product"

The core of an otome game is not gameplay, not technology, not even content quality — it is the one-to-one emotional contract between player and virtual character. Players invest not just money but sustained emotional projection. Papergames treated the new male lead as a "commercial expansion tool" (Western hard-masculine style = targeting overseas markets), but players experienced him as "an intruder in their emotional space."

### 6.2 The Arrogance of 84 Billion

2025 revenue of 84B RMB, 45% global YoY growth, staggering per-employee output — these numbers likely bred strategic arrogance in management. Papergames appeared to believe technical prowess and content capability could主导 (steer/dictate) player emotional orientation, while neglecting底线把控 (baseline/red-line governance) during global expansion.

### 6.3 The Vacuum in Content Review

"A-0731" file code, registrar name "Yan Song," English name "Valko" — if one were coincidence, three simultaneously pointing in the same direction strains credibility. This reveals a serious缺失 (absence) in content review mechanisms and insufficient historical sensitivity among team members.

### 6.4 Cumulative Eruption

This crisis was not an isolated incident. From the 2025 battle system collapse, PV false-advertising lawyer letters, account theft wave, to the CV replacement controversy — player distrust accumulated incrementally. Ao Yin was merely the straw that broke the camel's back.
